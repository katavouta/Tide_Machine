Origins of the Selected Constituents

  • M2 (Principal lunar semi-diurnal): Dominant constituent forced by the Earth-Moon rotation.
  • S2 (Principal solar semi-diurnal): Main solar component, interacting to cause spring-neap variations.
  • N2 (Larger lunar elliptic): Accounts for the monthly variation in lunar distance.
  • K1 (Lunisolar diurnal): Adjusts for the declination tilts of both the Moon and Sun.
  • O1 (Principal lunar diurnal): Captures the changing north-south path of the lunar orbit.
  • M4 (Shallow water overtide): Local distortion generated in shallow shelf seas.
