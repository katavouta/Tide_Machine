Origins of the Selected Constituents

  • M2: Principal lunar semi-diurnal.
  • S2: Principal solar semi-diurnal.
  • N2: Larger lunar elliptic.
  • K2: Lunisolar semi-diurnal.
  • K1: Lunisolar diurnal.
  • O1: Principal lunar diurnal.
  • P1: Solar diurnal. Closely linked to K1, it accounts for the solar influence on the diurnal cycle.
  • M4 & M6: Shallow water higher harmonics.
